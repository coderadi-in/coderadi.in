the brief

timeline

Back in January 2026, when I was in 11th grade, I shipped my first client project. It was a small web application with a few utility tools — things like a PDF cropper and a calculator.

I've spent more than a couple of weeks on that projects, fixing bugs, pushing upgrades, making every bit of the app better everyday, the project was shipped but I was just about to be expelled from the school.

This project got me a small payment of INR 5K, which was splitted equally to me and my friend who helped me close the first client.

It was the first time I felt I did something, a massive load of success, it wasn't a small number of me, but it is the beginning of something great.

chapter 1

HOW WE CLOSED THE FIRST CLIENT

The first we closed was one of the leading E-com mentor in India, my friend with his friendly talking behavior, partnered with mentor.

We were supposed to build the application for him without development charges, and in return he was sending us leads of his students, it was great.

I have put all of my knowledge and experience to build that, I made every bit of the app better. The first minimal version was ready, a web-application with PDF cropping, and two different types of calculators.

The client paid for the server bills.

The next day, our parents got a call from call teacher saying that he won't give us internal marks in the exam.

chapter 2

HANDLING ALL AT ONCE

We went to school the next, after the gap of more than 15 days, the class teacher scolded us, as one do.

He said that we won't give us internal marks, but we're in full confidence and we were not feeling low, coz we just got money from a third person.

Our parents said to go school everyday without any gap, they told to not do our work, but now we were not listening to anyone.

That evening, we got a call from the client, he was very satisfied with the app and want some more tools in it.

Fortunately, we got three holidays in a row, for some school function, that three days went hard, working hours to upgrade the client app.

chapter 3

WE GOT SOMETHING WHO'LL FIGHT FOR US

After working hours for three holidays, the shipment of v3 of the app was done. That evening the client paid us INR 5,000.

He said that he won't make us work for free. This was the result that my skills aren't going vain.

I and my friend went to a restaurant, had a cup of coffee, relaxed for a bit, then we went to a nearby ATM and fetched out the money we made in cash.

The ATM was recently refilled and the cash was looking brand new, I went to my father and shown the cash.

Now, no one was saying me to go school daily, don't skip a single day.

That's it? Perhaps not. Coz, after the shipment was done. I again got a pause, however I got something that could resume it, THE REPETITION OF EXPOSURE TO REJECTION.
