a brief of mine

Hi, I'm Aditya (Aditya Singh), a web developer and entrepreneur from Uttar Pradesh, India.

For the past three years, I've been exploring the world of development and digital business. I work with personal brands, e-commerce businesses, and online coaches to build clean, minimal websites and small digital systems that make their work easier and more scalable.

Interestingly, my first payment, first appreciation, and first real encouragement came from a stranger on the internet. At the same time, my first doubts and “this won't work” comments came from people close to me. That contrast quietly shaped how I see things — value is ultimately decided by the people you serve.

Since then, I've been focused on improving my skills, experimenting with ideas, and building simple systems that actually help people do their work better.
